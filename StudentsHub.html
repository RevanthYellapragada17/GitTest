Transportation

I-Ride

The I-ride services is free for students within the campus and charges
2$ per drop location. Call the Iride number and signup to book easily on mobile app.
Tip:Remember to plan atleast 1 hour in advance as Iride is not an instant service.



Essentials

Dollar Tree

Dollar Tree is a budget-friendly store where international students can find affordable essentials, from groceries to household items. It helps students stretch their limited budgets while settling into a new environment. The wide variety of products allows them to access everyday necessities without overspending.



Walmart

Walmart offers international students a one-stop shop for affordable groceries, electronics, and household goods. Its extensive selection and competitive prices help students save money while meeting all their needs in one place. With 24/7 locations in many areas, Walmart provides convenience for busy student schedules.



Aldi

Aldi is ideal for international students seeking high-quality groceries at low prices. With a focus on fresh produce and essentials, Aldi helps students maintain a healthy diet on a budget. Its streamlined shopping experience and low-cost, private-label brands make it an excellent choice for cost-conscious students.
